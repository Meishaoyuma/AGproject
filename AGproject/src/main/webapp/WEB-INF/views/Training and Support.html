Training and Support

Trimble Ag offers a wide variety of options for resellers and customers to learn how to effectively use our products. Everyone learns differently so we strive to offer a variety of educational mediums for everyone. Learn more about our training and support options below.

E-learning

Software customers can search printable step-by-step guides, watch videos, download desktop software updates, and get workflows for Trimble Ag Software atHow-To Central. Find older versions of the desktop Trimble Ag Software and Farm Works Software® at this link. Finally, software customers can find the latest software updates and enhancements here.

Hardware and software customers can also study at their own pace on Learn.Trimble.com where they’ll find a variety of e-learning modules. Visit Learn.Trimble.com to register or login.

Training Classes

Software online training is available via weekly webinars. Customers are emailed invitations after new purchases of Farmer Fit, Farmer Pro, and Advisor Prime. These training sessions are available in English, expanding to other languages in the future.

Customer Support

Hardware customers should reach out to their reseller for support questions. Click here to connect with your local Trimble reseller.

Software customers can contact their reseller for support or send an email through Trimble Ag Software following the steps below:

  • Login to Trimble Ag Software
  • Click on the question mark in the upper right corner
  • Click on Email Support
  • Fill in the form to send your question to Support
